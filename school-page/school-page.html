CODERVA REGIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Computer Science Based School

I attend CodeRVA Regional High School, which is a computer science based school. CodeRVA encourages agile workplace methodologies, scrum, self management, and project-based learning. For example, Capital One employees visit once in a while and engage students in activities related to scrum.There are also fun activities that promote logical thinking, such as, Puzzle Day, and Computer Science Week. CodeRVA provides an opportunity to earn an associates degree in Computer Science, or to earn college credits that can be transferred. One of the dual enrollment classes offered is a class in Java. They have electives that range from, cybersecurity, programming, video game design, and web design.

They also have internships which are done during the 11th and 12th grade year with Maxx Potential. The internship gives students experience in cybersecurity, data, and development. There are multiple jobs given for each module. For example, for cybersecurity, I was the system administrator; For data, I was the policy administrator; And for development, I was the software developer.
